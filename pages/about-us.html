About TradeRisk Calculator

TradeRisk Calculator is a simple yet powerful web app designed to help traders manage their risk effectively. By combining Stop Loss %, Risk %, and Capital, the tool instantly shows you the dollar amount at risk and the required margin for your trade.

Our Mission

We believe that discipline is the foundation of successful trading. TradeRisk Calculator helps traders stay consistent by removing guesswork, preventing costly mistakes, and encouraging better money management.
